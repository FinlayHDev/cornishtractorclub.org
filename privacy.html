Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

The Cornish Tractor Club ("us", "we", or "our") operates the website cornishtractorclub.org (the "Website"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information we receive from users of the Website.

The Cornish Tractor Club is committed to protecting the privacy of our website visitors. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Personal Data We Collect

When you visit our website, we may collect certain personal data, such as your IP address, device information, and browsing history. We may also collect personal data if you submit a contact form or sign up for our newsletter.

Use of Personal Data

We use the personal data we collect for the following purposes:

To provide and improve our website and services.
To respond to your inquiries and requests.
To send you newsletters and other promotional materials (if you have opted-in).
To comply with legal obligations.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies, such as cross-cookies, Facebook features (which can include cookies), iframes, Google Analytics, and Cloudflare, to improve our website and services. Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website.

We use cookies to:

Analyze website traffic and usage patterns.
Remember your preferences and settings.
Authenticate and secure your access to our website.
You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, please note that disabling cookies may affect your ability to access certain features of our website.

Beacons

Beacons, also known as pixel tags, web beacons or clear GIFs, are small images or pieces of code that are embedded on web pages, videos or emails. We may use beacons to track the actions of users of our website, to explore how the site is being used, and to compile statistics about usage of our website.

Beacons can collect information such as your IP address, browser type, and the time and date of your visit.

We may use beacons from third-party service providers, such as Google Analytics, to help us analyze how users interact with our website. These service providers may use information about your visits to our website and other sites to provide you with targeted ads.

If you would like to opt-out of the collection of information through beacons or other tracking technologies, you can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent. However, please note that some parts of our website may not function properly if you disable cookies or other tracking technologies.

Data Security

The security of your Personal Information is important to us, but no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

International Transfer

Your information, including Personal Information, may be transferred to and maintained on computers located outside of your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where data protection laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction.

If you are located outside the United Kingdom and choose to provide information to us, please note that we transfer the information, including Personal Information, to the United Kingdom and process it there.

Your consent to this Privacy Policy followed by your submission of such information represents your agreement to that transfer.

Links to other sites

Our website may contain links to other websites that are not operated by us. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that third party's site. We strongly advise you to review the privacy policy of every site you visit. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies or practices of any third-party sites or services.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, rectify, and delete personal data we hold about you. You also have the right to object to our processing of your personal data, and to withdraw your consent (if applicable). To exercise these rights, please contact us using the contact details provided below.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us at website@cornishtractorclub.org.
