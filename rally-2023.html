Rally 2023

Overview

This year's rally is already set to be a big one, with Ken Fox's "Wall of Death" joining, bringing a completely new attraction to the space. We've also got Lawn Mower racing within the grounds which is set to be a great attraction, both within the more extreme side after the Cornish Tractor Pullers joined us last year!
We've also got a all new exhibit category, we warmly welcome the vintage caravans to the grounds adding to our already broad list of exhibits!

As always, we have attractions to entertain those of all ages, if its children taking on the "Tractor Tug-of-War" or take yourself on a tour, exploring the grounds for vehicles, the working section or stands we are sure there will be plenty of attractions to fit your taste!
